Daniel Pilon's Website

My name is Daniel Pilon. I created this website for a web design course at St. Paul College, where I am currently working towards a degree in Computer Science. In addition to hosting my resume, it also hosts some of my art and web pages I've created. I'm always striving to improve and expand my knowledge of web development, and I'm excited to see what my future holds in this field.

I live and work in St. Paul, Minnesota. I was born in 1995, and at time of writing, I am 28 years old. My preferred pronouns are they/them. In my spare time, I enjoy writing, video games, mathematics, and making art and music.

I prefer not to share photos of myself online; instead, I have attached a photo of my cat Teddy. I have other cats, but he is the most photogenic.
